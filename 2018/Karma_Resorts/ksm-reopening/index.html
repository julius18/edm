On 28th March 2018 Karma St. Martin’s comes alive again, reopening its doors to guests, islanders and day-trippers alike from Easter weekend onwards. The team will continue to be led by Bjoern Duerr and Scott Fisher, whose experience, passion and drive will ensure we sustain the excellence Karma St. Martin’s visitors have come to know and expect.  Cricket star Darren Gough will be cutting the ribbon, so please come and join us for a glass of bubbles!

Festivities will begin with our legendary Curry Night at The Cloudesley Shovell Restaurant, where Executive Head Chef Mike Potts is already preparing his signature gourmet Hog Roast for Good Friday to ensure our cuisine is the best in Scilly for the fourth consecutive year.

Karma St. Martin’s will be wrapping up the festivities with our highly popular Sunday roast as well as an Easter Egg Hunt, in which one lucky winner will get to enjoy a second holiday at Karma Bavaria in Germany.  How’s that for good Karma?!

Karma St. Martin’s will remain open until 31st October 2018, with a host of activities and events planned throughout the season, including luxury swimming holidays, our second annual Jazz Festival, top-notch music acts, and foraging gourmet dinners throughout the year.

 
 

Book your Easter holiday soon as we expect to be very busy over this period. You’ll be pleasantly surprised with the Spring Savings available. We can’t wait to welcome you back to Karma St. Martin’s for an eggstra special stay!
