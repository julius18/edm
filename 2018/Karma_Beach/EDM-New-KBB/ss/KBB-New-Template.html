EXPERIENCE BALI

Sand's Sound

DJ royalty Mark Doyle kicks up the sand at Karma Beach Bali for Sand's Sound this October 7, 2018 from 11 am - 11 pm.

Doyle's numbers make for some staggering reading; the creator of 3 best-selling dance brands (Hed Kandi, Stereo Sushi and Fierce Angel), a DJ for over 25 years, compiler of over 80+ best-selling albums resulting in over 3 million sales plus a phenomenal ear for a hit record with 3 top 10 UK chart records and another ten top 10 club chart hits under his belt.

Dance to Doyle's signature brand of House and take part in sand arts, beach games, Special Tapas and seaside cocktails!

Supported by Dan Cook.

Entry Rp. 500,000 including Rp. 300,000 food & beverage credit
