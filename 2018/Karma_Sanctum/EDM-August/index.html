Rock Cab Tours and Wild Heart Afternoon Tea
www.sanctumsoho.com/restaurant

The Karma Sanctum Soho Hotel has collaborated with Rock Cab Tours to bring you a Rock’n’Roll afternoon consisting of a guide to the most famous rock landmarks in music history followed by a delicious Afternoon Tea in our venue.

The hugely successful Rock Cab Tours are a two-hour ride that “meanders through the city’s backstreets to pivotal landmarks that capture British music’s impact across decades. An exploration of London’s musical history with a tour guide in the form of former Warner exec Stephen Channell” (as featured in the Metro).

Your guide, Stephen Channell, is a veteran of the music industry, spending 25 years working for Warner Music and the BBC. Now a qualified black cab driver, no one knows the streets and rock history of London like Stephen does.

The Tour will take you to the locations of David Bowie’s albums, The Beatles Abbey Road studios (including the famous zebra crossing of course) as well as other famous landmark destinations for Jimi Hendrix, Queen, Marc Bolan, Oasis and the Rolling Stones.

After your tour you will be treated to an Afternoon Tea at our rock’n’roll restaurant, Wild Heart Bar & Grill. Enjoy a selection of scones and sweets, finger sandwiches and a selection of teas to complete your afternoon against a backdrop of artwork and a great soundtrack.

The full package is £99 and can be purchased by emailing haleh@sanctumsoho.com or for more information on the tour, please contact info@rockcabtours.com


 
ART EXHIBITION: REBOOT BY SHIV GREWAL
Thursday, 16th August – Monday, 24th September, displayed daily

“Reboot” at The Sanctum Hotel, London, is the latest exhibition from Indian-born London-based artist Shiv Grewal, whose life was smashed to pieces when his near-death experience of a cardiac arrest left him in a month-long coma five years ago.

Reboot is a rare insight into the oldest and most profound question of all; what happens when we die …? Highly reflective digital artworks printed on aluminium powerfully capture Shiv’s remarkable story and vision that ‘this world is just a fraction of what there is’. He strives to capture the shapes and experiences he encountered during his coma and in his ongoing recovery, and reconcile them with his everyday life.

We will be displaying the works in our restaurant, Wild Heart Bar & Grill, daily from Thursday 16th August to Monday 24th September.

View Shiv’s Instagram here: www.instagram.com/artshivinder
