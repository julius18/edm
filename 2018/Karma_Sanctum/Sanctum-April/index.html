Sanctum Soho Cigar Club
19th April

7pm-9pm

Karma Sanctum Soho are delighted to announce the arrival of ‘Sanctum Soho Cigar Club’.

The Soho Cigar Club was set up by Stephan von Schilling following a conversation in Havana in 2015. The concept was simple: allow someone to enjoy a great cigar, in the right environment and with the right people and a certain magic ensues.

Tastings do not need to have long introductory speeches. There does not need to be a sense of formality other than the respect of fellow club members. We think you will love it

The launch scheduled for April 19th will allow members, ambassadors and VIP’s to enjoy a range of cigars whilst sampling delicious canapes and cocktails. The Cigar club aims to create a hub where members can relax, socialize and smoke the best cigars sourced from around the world.

Cigars are also available for purchase from our bespoke humidor and can be paired with Cognacs, rums and whiskey.

For more information and how to join this exclusive club please send an email to contact@sohocigarclub.co.uk

Cocktail of the month…
This month’s top cocktail is our latest edition, we call it “Ghost Rider”, featuring Ghost Vodka the newest member to our ever growing bar shelves.

How to make:
50ml Ghost Vodka shaken with 25ml Luxardo Maraschino and 25ml strawberry puree, squeeze in half a fresh lime, add a shot of pineapple juice and a dash of simple syrup. Shake with ice and serve with fresh lime and fresh strawberry for a delicious refreshing fruity number.
