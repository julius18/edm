With a stunning Restaurant, secluded Cinema Room and our much sought-after Rooftop Terrace, Karma Sanctum Soho Hotel is the ideal venue to host a wide variety of private events, tailor made to your needs by our team. Whether it’s an elegant dinner, wedding reception, birthday to remember or corporate event, we have a dedicated and experienced team to make your occasion unforgettable.

Located on our fifth floor, the intimate Roof Terrace is our secret hub complete with it’s own private bar, heated indoor area and garden area with a jacuzzi. Our full drinks list is available along with finger food and bowl food menus.

Our vintage-glamour themed cinema room is a multi-purpose hub. The perfect option for screening events, it is fully equipped with screening facilities and is sound-proofed. Boasting a private bar, the cinema can also be transformed into a space for private dining, parties, meetings, product launches, wine tastings and performances.

Wild Heart, our hotel’s in-house Restaurant, Bar and Lounge, is available for large group bookings as well as private hires – we have a number of menus available for groups, including three-course offerings with indulgent dishes from our gourmet-inspired kitchen or selections of tasty canapes and finger food. The space can also be hired for a variety of occasions, from wedding receptions and Bar Mitzvahs to Christmas parties and corporate events.

With all events spaces DJ equipment can be provided at no extra charge, and a DJ can be sourced for an additional cost.

 

To discuss any of these options in more detail, please contact events@sanctumsoho.com to speak to our professional and helpful team, who will be happy to assist in arranging the perfect event for you.

Capacities
ROOFTOP TERRACE : Up to 75 standing
CINEMA : Up to 70 seated or 100 standing
RESTAURANT : Up to 80 seated or 200 standing
