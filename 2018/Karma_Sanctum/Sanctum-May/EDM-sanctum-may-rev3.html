May the warmth be with you
As we approach the beginning of Summer, there’s plenty of reasons to celebrate and fill your diary – why not relax on our luxurious rooftop terrace with a cocktail and fine Cuban cigar.

For those who love a party, we’ve got the perfect event lined up for you – it’s time to pick up the tequila shots, as our Cinco de Mayo Fiesta will kick off on Saturday, 5th of May. For those not ready to end the celebrations, you can continue the good vibes into the following day, as our Sunday Bottomless Lunch has arrived complete with a full roast and endless cocktails.

Continue reading to find out more about our exciting May offerings and events or email haleh@sanctumsoho.com for further details.

We look forward to welcoming you!


 
Sunday Bottomless Roast at Wild Heart Bar & Grill
Every Sunday: 11.30am – 3pm

Want to perk up your weekend and carry on the good vibes? Get stuck into an epic two-course Sunday Roast with all the trimmings along with bottomless cocktails at Wild Heart Bar & Grill.

Kicking off the feast is a selection of freshly baked bread and toppings including smashed avocado, lobster butter and more, followed by your main meal: enjoy a 30-day aged rib of beef with Yorkshire puddings, veggies and gravy, or go for one of our brunch options such as Croque Monsieur or Eggs Benedict whilst sipping free-flowing cocktails (choose from infused gin or vodka and elderflower le fizz) - and the lot is for only £35!

Cinco de Mayo Fiesta at Karma Sanctum Soho Hotel
Saturday, 5th May: 5pm - midnight

Think skulls, sombreros and tequila as the theme for our Sanctum Saturday Session - our regular Saturday Happy Hour will be extended from 5pm all the way through to 9pm, with 2-4-1 cocktails at £14, and a live set from DJ Ray Noir from 7pm until midnight. Everyone is welcome to enjoy our Happy Hour and to join the party, and £20 tickets will get you a Margarita, El Diablo and mini tacos in true Cinco de Mayo style.


 
Cocktail of the Month
This month’s top cocktail is the floral and refreshing Lazy Lavender, inspired by the upcoming Chelsea Flower Show. Celebrate the arrival of Spring with some after-work Happy Hour cocktails in our restaurant, lounge and bar, exclusively offered at two for £14.
