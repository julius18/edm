Ace of Spades Afternoon Tea
We’ve revamped our Afternoon Tea as part of our exciting new seasonal menu changes for Autumn, and our brand new Ace of Spades offering features a delightful selection of both savoury and sweet treats to feast on. Departing from tradition, our new Afternoon Tea hosts a range of decadent meat dishes and puddings to fill you up, along with fine and classic teas. Indulge in a tender steak sandwich, beef slider and scotch egg, followed by our famous mandarin cheesecake and Eton mess. Complete your afternoon with a choice of a Cosmpolitan cocktail or glass of Gentleman Jack with a cigar on our roof terrace.

Afternoon Tea is available Sunday – Friday between 3 – 5pm, or 4 – 6pm on Saturdays, book by emailing our restaurant or on bookatable: https://www.bookatable.co.uk/wild-heart-restaurant-at-sanctum-soho-hotel-soho-chinatown-london
