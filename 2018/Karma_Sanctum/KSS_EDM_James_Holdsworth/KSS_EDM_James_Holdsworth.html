THE CHELSEA HOTEL, 222 WEST 23RD NYC
COCKTAIL RECEPTION MONDAY 14TH MAY, 6 – 8PM
EXHIBITION: 14TH MAY – 3RD JULY

We’re delighted to be hosting an art exhibition once again of the works of James Holdsworth, following the huge success of his last show at the Karma Sanctum Soho Hotel. Join us for a cocktail reception and exclusive preview of his works held on Monday 14th May.

Holdsworth’s paintings are a mixture of humour, horror and harmony: each featuring a different popular character as a protagonist surrounded by a deep, murky and unevaluated background. Iconographic images are here the presentation of a dream world where access to “happiness” appears to be part of an ordinary life.

To realise this complex urban background Holdsworth selects special wallpapers, articles collected from recycled newspapers and posters from cities he visits, including Los Angeles, New York, Paris, London. He also uses archive paper taken from old tour posters; the otherworldliness of rock and roll and Hollywood are seen from an artist’s perspective.

Holdsworth’s upcoming exhibition, about the iconic, infamous Chelsea Hotel NYC, will be displayed in our in-house restaurant, Wild Heart Bar & Grill, from 14th May through to 3rd July.
