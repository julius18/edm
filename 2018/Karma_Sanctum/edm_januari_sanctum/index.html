Art Exhibition DanteVon and IamJii
January 24th
6pm-8pm

Join us at Wild Heart for a complimentary cocktail reception and to view the work of IamJii and DanteVon.

‘Avec toi, coup d'etat’ (With you, coup d'etat) ; a collaboration between IamJii and DanteVon.

IamJii is both a writer and illustrator who is best known for her beautifully surreal and narrative intricate Folklore drawings. Originally from South Korea she studied at Kingston University, taking a particular interest in Neo-Romanticism and drawing inspiration from John Piper, Paul Nash and Carl Jung. Nikos is a global citizen, and actor whose creative centre has thrived on the various cultures he has experienced.

Nikos (DanteVon) first met Jihyun (IamJii) in 2014 in a café on Kingsland Road and after falling in love they have travelled the world together ever since. The path of an artist can lay in solitude, but Avec toi, coup d’etat is all about duality, two different worlds combining as one. Creating In a fusion of styles : expressionism, abstract and impressionism, using acrylic, ink , line drawing, watercolor, and pastels.
