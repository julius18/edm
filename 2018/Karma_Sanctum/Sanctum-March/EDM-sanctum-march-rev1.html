Savage Communication art exhibiton
March 21st

6-8pm

Join us for a cocktail reception at Wild Heart Bar and Grill for the opening night of Savage Communication exhibition.

Marco Palumbo-Rodrigues is a London-based artist and graphic designer. He has also co-run No Front Teeth Records, a punk rock record label, since 2000 as well as singing in numerous bands.

Martin Clark started his career in art working at Popworld Promotions specialising in cult bands and alternative T-shirts from the Punk, Rockabilly and Psychobilly music scenes. He is also an accomplished musician. Martin started tattooing at 21 and is now the owner of Bluebird Tattoo in Watford. His tattoos and art have appeared on record sleeves, clothing brands and in many publications.

Savage Communication is a true collaboration – Marco and Martin work on everything together; from the initial concepts to the final execution.

It should be noted that they consider Rock ‘n’ Roll to be very extensive and not just reserved for music-related subject matter but also film stars, cartoon characters, fictional characters and anything that they feel represents Rock ‘n’ Roll for them.
