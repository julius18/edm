JUNE: THE GATEWAY TO SUMMER
We hope you have been enjoying the glorious weather – now that Summer is upon us, there’s certainly plenty of reasons to celebrate.

Throughout the month we have lots of events and offers lined up to keep you entertained, including the streaming of the FIFA World Cup on our roof terrace with a brand new barbeque bites menu. We are delighted to announce our collaborations with Moet & Chandon: Generosity Moments, a complimentary glass served with every glass ordered through June, followed by our biggest collaboration yet, the Rooftop Summer Sessions party in July.

Our hugely popular Bottomless Brunches on Saturdays and Sundays will continue, and on Sunday 17th June our exclusive Father’s Day menu with two courses and a half a bottle of wine will be a new offering for the special day.

We hope to welcome you at the Karma Sanctum Soho Hotel this month at one of our exclusive events!


 
In Association with Moet: Summer Rooftop Sessions and Generosity Moments
Saturday, July 7th: 12:00 – 18:00

Summer in London is always best celebrated enjoying daytime drinks in the sun atop a rooftop. We have the perfect event lined up in partnership with Moet on Saturday, 7th July - an exclusive party on our secret rooftop terrace, complete with London’s top DJ spinning on deck and to top it off we have a special surprise. Don’t forget to bring your best swimsuit for a quick dip in the jacuzzi! Entry tickets are just £20 and you will be served a bottle of Moet & Chandon per two persons on arrival.

In the build up to the party we will be offering Generosity Moments throughout the entire month of June: for every glass of Moet champagne ordered in our bar, a second will be served complimentary!

 
Exclusive Father’s Day Menu at Wild Heart Bar & Grill
Sunday, June 17th: 11:30am – 3pm

It’s only natural to take the time on Father’s Day to show Dad how special he is, so why not ditch the patterned socks and ties and treat him to a delicious lunch to remember?

At Wild Heart Bar & Grill, we will be offering an exclusive Father’s Day menu for just £22.50, along with half a bottle of red or white wine. Tuck into a selection of freshly baked bread to start, with toppings such as avocado smash and lobster butter, followed by a selection of dishes that include brunch classics like croque monsieur or the all-important traditional Sunday Roast, complete with Yorkshire puddings, veggies and gravy.

To top off the meal, why not visit our rooftop terrace and enjoy a Cuban cigar from our fine selection, with a glass of whiskey. All dads will also receive a special gift on the day.


 
World Cucumber Week
Thursday, June 14th – Wednesday, June 20th

World Cucumber Day on 14th June is the annual celebration of the cucumber, which is infused in everybody’s favourite gin, Hendrick’s. Claim a complimentary glass of Hendrick’s & Tonic throughout the week at our restaurant, Wild Heart Bar & Grill, by heading over to Hendrick’s Facebook page, www.facebook.com/HendricksGinUk, and following the instructions. You’ll be issued with a “Cucumber Exchange” voucher to bring over to us and will be presented with your complimentary drink. Don’t forget to hashtag #WorldCucumberDay!
