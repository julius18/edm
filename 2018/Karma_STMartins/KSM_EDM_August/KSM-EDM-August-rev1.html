What a Cracking Summer!

The team here at Karma St. Martin’s are delighted to have soldiered through August which is always the busiest (and most fun!) time in Scilly. We were delighted to see so many familiar faces returning to the Isles for their summer holidays; it’s one of the best aspects of our job, meeting wonderful people again and again! We’re sincerely thankful for everyone who holidayed with us this season, and we look forward to seeing more of you in September and October.
