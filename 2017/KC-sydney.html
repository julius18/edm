Hook double passes to the Sydney 7’s Rugby

Karma Group has a long and proud association International Rugby Union and this February we’re in the mood to celebrate. Not only have we renewed our support as Official Resort Partner for Super Rugby team, the Western Force (11 years and counting) - we’ve also just secured talented scrum-half, TJ Perenara, as an official Karma Resorts ambassador! You may just catch him lounging poolside at one of our many properties when he’s not playing for the Hurricanes or New Zealand’s mighty national squad, the All Blacks.

In the U.K, Karma’s involvement with the Rosslyn Park National School 7s tournament helps to nurture English rugby’s stars of tomorrow, while down in Western Australia our partnership with the RugbyWA Juniors is inspiring the next generation of Wallabies… you could say we have both hemispheres covered.
