The secret to Indonesian cuisine


Ask any Indonesian chef worth their salt and they'll tell you - the secret to great Indonesian cuisine is in the sauce.

Indonesian Chef, Wayan Eddie, agrees. As Head Chef of our Karma Reef resort, set on the idyllic island of Gili Meno, Eddie excels at recreating the flavours and textures of the Indonesian archipelago on the plate and palate.

Hailing from nearby Bali - and having worked for some of the most prestigious resorts island-wide - Eddie's style is a mix of contemporary and accessible Indonesian food. Don't miss Karma Reef's take on the Rendang Sapi: rich slow - cooked beef in thick and fragrant spiced gravy - outstanding!

While Eddie and the team sate your taste buds, the rest of Karma Reef's friendly staff is proud to offer you sunset sets from internationally acclaimed DJ's, imaginatively original cocktail creations and daily island tours. Or, you could simply relax with a beachside massage at our onsite Karma Spa. It's the ultimate barefoot escape!

Explore Sanur - Bali's largest traditional village

A mere 30 minutes from Bali's Ngurah Rai Denpasar International Airport, Karma Royal Sanur is the perfect base from which to explore one of the most stately and established areas of traditional Bali. Sanur plays host to a wealth of cultural, dining and outdoor experiences. Discover Bali's efforts at marine life conservation in an undersea adventure with the Bali Seawalker. Take a walk along the ocean floor while you view the island's abundant fish life, using a specially constructed helmet.

Back on land, take the time to explore the deeply personal art of Adrien-Jean Le Mayeur de Merpres, a Brussels-born artist who spent his life painting the two things he loved most - Bali and its women. His muse, a Legong dancer named Ni Pollok, eventually married Merpres and features heavily in his work.

A Byzantine Easter tradition like no other

Every year on the Cycladic island of Paros, during Good Friday, the small village of Marpissa thrums with the footfall of thousands of people in quiet procession there to observe a unique series Byzantine tableaux vivants. Depicting the series of biblical events leading up to Jesus's death and resurrection - including the last supper - locals from Marpissa and the surrounding villages craft and 'act' out astonishingly intricate scenes.

Increasingly popular among international tourists, there's never been a better time to discover this unique event for yourself. Luckily Karma can help - our global partner network gives Karma Club members access to the Anezina Village resort. Home to beautiful gardens (and we hear, an amazing Greek kitchen), Anezina is the perfect accommodation for your Greek Easter adventure.

Pennsylvania an outdoor adventurer's paradise

Swimming, hiking, fishing, all season tubing, rope courses, zip lines, horseback riding, golf and snow tubing… that's just a sample of the outdoor activities you can tuck into at The Villas at Tree Tops in Bushkill, Pennsylvania USA.

Visit the State where the Declaration of Independence was signed - home to the Liberty Bell, the Hersey's Chocolate factory and a wealth of American history. Explore the vast wilderness of the Appalachian Mountains and hike through mist-filled valleys, exploring the native flora and fauna.
