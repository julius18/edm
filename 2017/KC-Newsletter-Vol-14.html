BRITISH ROYALS WILLIAM & KATE VISIT KARMA ST. MARTINS!



It was a huge honour for the Karma St. Martin's team to welcome the British Royal Family's most beloved couple, Prince William and Catherine Duchess of Cambridge last week. The pair were on a tour of the Scilly Isles and arrived at Karma St. Martins by boat on 2nd of September, where they were welcomed with flowers. The visit has shone the spotlight on one of England's most delightful secrets - just a few miles off the Cornish coast, the Scilly Isles are characterized by undulating green landscapes and white beaches reminiscent of the tropics, plus native wildlife including seals, puffins and even beautiful dolphins. Karma St. Martins provides luxurious accommodation in a rustic English cottage style with a wide range of rooms and suites to cater from everyone from honeymoon couples to families. Besides the gorgeous scenery, activities include snorkeling, trekking, diving, sailing and windsurfing - or simply picnicking on one of those picture postcard beaches.

THE SOUND OF (ROCK) MUSIC! KARMA BAVARIA GETS ITS GROOVE ON

Karma Bavaria may occupy a rural idyll on the shores of Lake Schliersee surrounded by Alpine peaks. But there are other types of rock to experience here too - namely rock n roll! Nearby Munich is a city famed for its arts and entertainment offerings - from major gatherings like the famous Rockavaria rock festival each May to the city's popular Rock Museum in a vertical tower high above the city, which hosts rolling exhibitions and loads of memorabilia. Then there are all the many rock oriented bars which cater to the city's die-hard rockers. Just ask your Karma Concierge or the staff at Karma Bavaria for the lowdown on the best the city has to offer when it comes to rocking out!

RECONNECT WITH YOUR CENTRE AT KARMA MAYURA, UBUD

Ubud Bali has long been a strong cultural, artistic and spiritual hub for the Balinese and that tradition has continued as this beautiful town in central Bali opened up to the world. Karma Mayura distills many of the most celebrated aspects of the area, offering guests a tranquil riverside retreat set in iridescent tropical gardens, impeccably decorated rooms featuring local arts & crafts and sophisticated renditions of local Balinese food. Ubud is just a few minutes away, offering a wide range of activities - it's home to some of the world's most highly regarded yoga teachers and study centres, art galleries that range from hole-in-the-walls to large museums, plus performances by the island's leading theatre and dance performers. When it comes to immersing in Bali's singular culture, Karma Mayura is the only passport you'll need.

PUERTO VALLARTA - MEXICAN MAGIC IN THE SIERRA MADRE

Set between the Sierra Madre mountains and spectacular Banderas Bay, Puerto Vallarta is arguably Mexico's prettiest resort town. Here you'll find the beautifully appointed Raintree's Club Regina - a hub of high living set just off one of the area's best beaches, with magnificent sea views. This is the perfect place to enjoy all those classic Mexican offering that have been taken up so enthusiastically all over the world - from authentic tacos and tangy guacamole to smooth Margaritas with a salty kick. Once a small fishing village, Puerto Vallarta retains its original charm, with traditional architecture jostling with the modern resorts lining the sea front. There are a host of shops, bars and galleries and you won't have to go far to see the art of one of the Mexico's most famous artist - Frida Kahlo's work can be seen in galleries, bars and restaurants throughout the city.
