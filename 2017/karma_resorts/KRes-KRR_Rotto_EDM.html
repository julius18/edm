Karma Resorts is proud to present the 2017 Karma Resorts Rottnest Channel Swim.  As one of the world’s great open water swimming events, the Rottnest Channel Swim pits swimmers’ spirit and endurance against the rugged blue majesty of the Indian Ocean. 

Starting from Cottesloe Beach, challengers swim an impressive 19.7km to Rottnest Island, Western Australia. Whether competing as a solo, duo or team, the Karma Resorts Rottnest Channel Swim is a ‘team’ event with all swimmers surrounded by a support network of paddlers, boat skippers and crew.

It’s a fun day out for the entire family – come and cheer on the challengers from the comfort of Karma Rottnest and enjoy our very special Seafood & Swim lunch! Don’t forget to slip, slop, slap – we’re partnering with the Cancer Council Western Australia to bring their message to entrants and the public on race day
