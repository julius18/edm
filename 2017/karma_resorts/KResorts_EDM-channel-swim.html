Karma Resorts is proud to present the 2017 Rottnest Channel Swim. As one of the world’s great open-water swimming events, the Rottnest Channel Swim pits swimmers’ spirit and endurance against the rugged blue majesty of the Indian Ocean.

Starting from Cottesloe Beach, challengers swim an impressive 19.7km to Rottnest Island, Western Australia. Whether competing as a solo, duo or team, the Karma Resorts Rottnest Channel Swim is a ‘team’ event - with all swimmers surrounded by a support network of paddlers, support craft and crew.

It’s a fun day out for the entire family but don’t forget to slip, slop, slap and protect yourself from the sun’s harmful effects. We’re partnering with the Cancer Council of Western Australia to bring their SunSmart message to entrants and the public on the day, we've also donated a team of four - along with a fully equipped boat, skipper, and professional swim coach - to the Cancer Council team, who will raise vital funds for cancer research and support for sufferers.
 

EVENT DETAILS

Race day kicks off early at Perth’s Cottesloe Beach on Saturday, 25th of February. Once the swimmers leave the beach, attention moves to Thomson Bay, Rottnest Island, for a full day of family-friendly fun!

Don’t forget to call by our finish line gazebo or the stunning Karma Rottnest resort, where we’ll be giving away special discounts, prizes, offers and more!
