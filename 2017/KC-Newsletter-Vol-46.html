Bali Kites Festival a must-see

As the winds from the Indian Ocean pick up during July, children and adults alike from all over Bali descend on the eastern coast of Padanggalak for the annual Bali Kites Festival.

What started as a seasonal festival to give thanks for the harvest has grown into a stunningly creative and visually spectacular yearly event. Kite designs become even more colourful each year, while there seems to be no limit to size! For the travel photographer, there's plenty of opportunity to capture that perfect shot.

Your stately private pool villa at Karma Jimbaran will be the perfect venue to relax after a day of aerial action. End the day with cocktails by the pool while our expert chefs prepare a sumptuous seafood BBQ.

Catch all the on and off court action of Wimbledon 2017

This UK summer, the world's tennis elite gather for the jewel in the crown of grass court tennis – Wimbledon.

While the event itself needs no introduction, we wouldn't want you to miss out on the electric vibe running through London this time of year. With the summer sun shining and the world coming to visit, London turns up the volume to eleven!

Catch all the action on and off court with a stay at the award-winning Karma Sanctum Soho, our brand new address located in central London and close to transport for those heading out to the All England Club.

Portugal artistically alight for Madeira Atlantic Festival

This June, Portugal comes alive for the annual Atlantic Festival in the island of Madeira. A combination of events – the Madeira Music Festival, an international fireworks competition, and various street performances – blend together to produce a colourful feast for the senses!

Complete with cosy apartments, pool and spa, the Pestana Village Resort is one of the best places on Madeira to stay while exploring all the festive action.
