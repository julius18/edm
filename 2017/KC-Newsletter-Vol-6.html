A KARMA ELYSIUM : MYKONOS

Mykonos is known as the Island of the Winds, for the strong gusts that have shaped its rocky terrain. But it's also an island of sunshine - 300 days of it a year! With a dramatic coastline that drops vertiginously into the sparkling waters of the Aegean, its iconic windmills and the charming adobe fishermen's cottages known as Little Venice, Mykonos is undeniably beautiful. It also boasts a sophisticated party scene famed throughout Europe, a reputation for high design, a thriving foodie scene and designer shopping, all of which make it one of Europe's most desirable island destinations. The island has long been one of the favoured destinations of Karma Group Chairman, John Spence and it is now home to one of Karma Club's most spectacular residences - Pelikanos is an opulent six bedroom property in classic white washed adobe and deep sea blue, perched on a cliff top with views of the neighbouring islands of Naxos and Delos, making it the perfect gateway to the island, especially for wedding parties and larger groups looking for a truly heavenly experience.

RIVIERA LIVING : LE PREVERGER

Few southern European destinations enjoy the mystique of the Cote d'Azur. The alluring mix of rolling lavender-scented provençal countryside, little medieval towns spilling down hillsides and one of the most famous harbor towns in the world - St. Tropez. This is the French Riviera - a magnet for celebrities, particularly during the Cannes film festival and those used to the finer things in life. This is the location of another of Karma Group's utterly magical residences. Le Preverger is a sprawling Chateau Estate set amid orchards and vineyards, yet whose interiors are surprisingly redolent of an English country cottage. This is because of its most famous former proprietor - the legendary English designer Laura Ashley. Le Preverger offers an experience of the Riviera never to be forgotten.
