Colors in London’s sky

This November, Bonfire Night will light the sky with colorful fireworks, illuminating London’s top attractions. The best epilogue to this magical night will be served at Karma Sanctum Soho’s Wild Heart Grill & Bar.

Find out more about Karma Sanctum Soho’s 5-star rock ‘n’ roll swagger here.

 

Balinese history unfolds

The annual Nusa Dua Fiesta offers deep insight into Balinese art and culture, featuring unique performances, intriguing art exhibitions and exciting sporting events. Immerse yourself in a colorful experience this November, enhancing it with soulful spas and traditional therapies at Karma Mayura.

 

Experience India’s contemporary culture scene
in New Delhi

India Art Festival provides both a fresh look at the country’s contemporary art scene and the meeting place for over 400 artists and connoisseurs. Take in art exhibitions, join free workshops and escape the frenetic way of modern life with a stay at Karma Royal Haathi Mahal.
 

Celebrate a Bavarian Martinmas

In Germany, St. Martin’s Day celebration is particularly popular with children, and is all about family bonding, whether singing around a huge bonfire, indulging in seasonal delicacies or engaging in traditional lantern-making.

Featuring indoor and outdoor activities - from the Three Monkeys Kids Club to winter sports in our idyllic alpine setting - Karma Bavaria provides the ideal setting for your family holidays.
