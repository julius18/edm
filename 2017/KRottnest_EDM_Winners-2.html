Congratulations to our Rottnest Channel Swim competition winner, Ms. Nicole O’Neill, and her team, “Nic’s Swimming Legends”! A long-time member of Karma’s Odyssey program, Nicole has won a place for herself and three mates in the 19.7km swim to Rottnest Island, Saturday 25th February!

We’d also like to congratulate Karma Club member Jay Walter, who won the secondary prize of a 7-night stay at Karma Kandara, our flagship resort in Bali!

While our new Karma Swim Team commences their training with our Karma Swim Coach, Paul Newsome of Swimsmooth Perth, he’s also taken the time to record a series of specialist training videos just for you.

OUR SUNSMART PARTNERSHIP WITH CANCER COUNCIL WA

We’re proud to announce our support for the Cancer Council of WA! Karma has donated a team of four - along with a fully equipped boat, skipper, and professional swim coach - to the Cancer Council team, who will raise vital funds for cancer research and support for sufferers. Team members Allison Hass, Chris Waldhart, James Strickland and Natalie Taylor are all hard at work training for their swim!

On race day, we’ll be helping the Cancer Council of WA to spread their SunSmart message - so remember to slip, slop, slap and support this much needed community organisation!
