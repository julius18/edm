Karma Kandara
You’ve never celebrated the festive season quite like this. Overlooking the blue expanse of the Indian Ocean and surrounded by the tropical magic of Bali, Karma Kandara brings you 12 Days of Karma - 12 days of curated experiences designed to delight the senses. Start a new tradition with a Christmas Day sunrise yoga session, or sample the best in Balinese and international fare daily. Celebrate sunset at Karma Beach Club, with sets by top DJ’s and daily festive cocktails.
 
 
 

Karma Jimbaran
Whether it is spa and wellness that jingles your bells or epicurean delights, to fanciful cultural excursions, Karma Jimbaran’s renowned 12 Days of Karma program is sure to leave a lasting memory for all this festive season.
 
 
 

Karma Reef
When palm trees replace mistletoe, and Christmas Day preparation is replaced by five-star festive feasts and merrily drinking in the sun to the best of tunes at our idyllic Karma Beach, Gili Meno. Karma Reef offers an utterly unforgettable setting in which to relax, recharge and indulge. Our resort chef gives their traditional festive menus the Gili Meno touch serving up a festive seafood selection and the freshest local produce for all to enjoy!
 
 
 

Karma Bavaria
With its picture-perfect alpine setting, Karma Bavaria offers natural beauty, extraordinary experiences and much-needed breathing space. You can ski or snowboard with the sun on your face, go ice-skating on Lake Schliersee or set off on a horse-drawn carriage ride, with mulled wine to keep you warm and cosy. Experience Karma this festive season in our winter wonderland.
