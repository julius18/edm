Western Australia celebrates under a beautiful, big sky

With a history spanning 50,000 years, Western Australia is one of the most spectacular and awe-inspiring places on earth.

On Monday June 5th, the people of WA celebrate Western Australia Day. Over on Rottnest Island, WA's most loved offshore escape, visitors and the community get together for BBQs, cultural events and cold beers.

A blooming good time on the Isles of Scilly
