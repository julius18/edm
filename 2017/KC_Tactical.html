Abounding in temples, spices, and exotic customs, Indochina is an incredible region. So is our offer, which serves up three iconic locations in Southeast Asia at impossibly low prices! Visit our resorts between now and 2018 to experience the best and most authentic of Thailand and Vietnam – through the signature luxury of Karma Group and the privilege of its Karma Club membership.
 
 

Karma Royal Bella Vista
Chiang Mai, Thailand

Nestled in the cultural heartland of northern Thailand amidst lush gardens and gentle streams, Karma Royal Bella Vista is an oasis of tranquility, comprised of thoughtfully-appointed hotel rooms in vernacular, home-style cottages right outside Chiang Mai. Explore the country’s spiritual side, as well as a plethora of cultural happenings and unique shopping opportunities in old town, just a shuttle ride away. Enjoy our restorative facilities, with world-class spa and swimming pool, and fuel up at our hotel restaurant, which serves authentic local fare. With our Three Monkeys Kids’ Club for the children and Karma Beach Club for parents, experience northern Thailand like royalty!
 
 

Travel Period: March – April, 2018
 
 

Karma Cây Tre
Hoi An, Vietnam

Set on Vietnam’s richly endowed central coast across the bridge from the elegant, historic town of Hoi An – now a UNESCO World Heritage Site – Karma Cây Tre is a fabulous new Karma Group resort constituting the perfect base from which to discover one of Southeast Asia’s most vibrant and diverse destinations. Overlooking lush tropical gardens, this idyllic riverside retreat consists of spacious, airy rooms enhanced by a premium spa, pool, poolside bar and white-sand Cua Dai Beach, plus unique activities such as our family cooking class. Immerse yourself in the exotic culture, cuisine and landscape of this fascinating region, informed by the stylish luxury of Karma Cây Tre.
 
 

Travel Period: November

 
 

Karma Royal Boat Lagoon
Phuket, Thailand

Phuket offers formidable insight into Thailand’s southern seaside culture, and a stay at Karma Royal Phuket is the best way to immerse yourself in its thriving arts, culture and foodie scene. Explore this extraordinary destination’s extravagance of beaches, parks and forests as well as its fantastic shopping, dining and nightlife. Situated in the “millionaires’ playground” of a yacht marina on the east of the island, Karma Royal Boat Lagoon is a respectable distance from the tourist hordes yet within easy access of all the island’s attractions. Endowed with a lagoon and top-notch facilities, including spa, clubhouse, swimming pool and fine-dining restaurants, this palm-fringed resort is a destination in and of itself.
 
 

Travel Period: November
