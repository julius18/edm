Raise a stein at Oktoberfest 2017

Every year the city of Munich shakes off the pending winter blues with that most delicious of beverages, beer.

The Bavarian Alps ring with the sound of revelry as Munich's beer halls through open their doors for 15-days of hop-laden fun.

Stay at Karma Bavaria, only 45 minutes from Munich's town centre.

Sanur celebrates

Join in all the colour and festive spirit of the Sanur Village Festival in Bali, this 9th-13th of August.

The festival combines five days of locally-inspired food, music, arts, and competitions, including Bali's famous Kite festival where thousands of kites take to the skies above the glistening shores of Bali's Sunrise Coast.

Stay at Karma Sanur, a cool oasis just a short walk from Sanur beach.
