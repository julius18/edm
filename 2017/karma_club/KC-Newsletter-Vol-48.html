Join in the laughs on the Isles of Scilly

Now in its third year, the Scilly Laughs Festival brings together some of the UK's best comic talent for a four day, four island comedy extravaganza this June 10th to 13th. This year features comedians Hannah Brackenbury, Richard Vranch and Craig Deeley, and all funds raised go to the Cornwall Air Ambulance service.

Situated on the picturesque island or St Martin's, Karma St Martin's offers sumptuously appointed accommodation and grounds, along with stunning views of the ocean, white sand beaches, and our award-winning Karma Spa.

Summer Lovin' on the isle of Crete

This July, just outside the village of Anogia, situated 1200m above sea level upon Crete's Psiloriti slopes, Crete celebrates the Hyakinthia. This celebration of Crete's very own saint of love, Aghios Hyakinthos, see locals and international guests alike treated to traditional cheese-making, folk music, crafts, and plenty of free-flowing raki!

Want to join? Stay at Karma Royal Villea. When not taking in the festival atmosphere, visit Crete's excellent beaches, ancient Minoan historical sites, and savour Cretan delicacies..

Perfume: A Sensory Journey Through Contemporary Scent

This summer, the Thameside arts institution at Somerset House, London, will bring together contemporary perfumers, who are experimenting with scent in new and exciting ways. The event will allow visitors to concoct their own scents in a perfume laboratory, as well as see the professionals at work.

Set a mere stone's throw away in the heart of central London, Karma Sanctum Soho offers guests award-winning luxury and access to all the dining, shows, shopping and history that make London one of the world's great cities.

Finland maximises the summer fun this August

This August, the world’s best creative talents descend on the Oulu August Festival in Finland - consisting of eight festivals and dozens of fringe events. The festival offers visitors a plethora of performances, including jazz, classical, visual arts, contemporary dance, experimental film and much more.

In Finland, stay at Holiday Club Pyhä, located in the splendour of Finland's Lapland region. Holiday Club Pyhä offers cosy holiday-style lofts and apartments, and a range of services including hiking, skiing, fishing, horse riding and plenty of fresh air.
