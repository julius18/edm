Trek one of India's tallest waterfalls

The Dudhsagar Falls – translated as the 'sea of milk' - is one of India's tallest waterfalls. Located along the Mandovi River in Goa, close to the border with Karnataka, the falls come alive during the monsoon every year when the Mandovi swells to a raging torrent.

No visit to Goa would be complete without a visit to this breathtaking site, replete with rich flora and fauna found in the surrounding forests. June to August is the best season to see Dudhsagar Falls in action. The friendly staff at Karma Royal Haathi Mahal are ready welcome you with their world-famous Goan hospitality.

Celebrate the Master Draught with the people of Rothenburg, Germany

The story goes like this. Back on the 31st of October 1631 the then Mayor of Rothenburg, George Nusch, saved the town from certain destruction by drinking down 3 and 1/4 litres of Franconian wine in a single gulp.

Fast forward to 2017 and this picturesque town in Bavaria, Germany, still celebrates with colourful reenactments of the 30 Years War. Our 5-star resort, Karma Bavaria, is located nearby Rothenburg and is the perfect place from which to explore this fairy-tale region of Germany.

Bali calls with colourful choirs

The 6th Bali International Choir Festival is on this 14th-20th July 2017 and sees choirs from across the world descend on the Island of the Gods for a mix of workshops, skills development, and of course public performance.

Located a stone's throw from many of the festival's main venues, Karma Royal Sanur is a peaceful oasis in Bali's bustling Sanur district. The resort's intimate restaurant, comfortable rooms and generously sized pool offer guests accessible luxury in a convenient location.

Experience Sicily's world-renowned Festival of the Arts

Hosted inside a stunning Greco-Roman amphitheatre in Taormina, Sicily's International Festival of the Arts is perhaps one of the top ways to experience southern Italy in summer. Running from June to August, the festival's crown is surely the Taormina Film Festival, occurring on the 10th-18th of June.

Continue your Italian summer sojourn from Sicilia to Puglia, with a stay at Residence Portoselvaggio, located on Puglia's western coast. Relax in comfortable 4 star holiday luxury with impressive amenities and, perhaps most importantly, the fresh paddock-to-plate flavours of Apulian cuisine!
