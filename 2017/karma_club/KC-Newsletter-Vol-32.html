Ski Bavaria with Karma

Let Karma’s cosy alpine resort, Karma Bavaria, be the base from which you explore the fairytale world of Bavarian culture, its natural wonders and rugged outdoors lifestyle.

Contact your Karma Concierge for more details about day tours to Munich - where you can down a thirst-quenching stein of Märzen - or cross the nearby Austrian border and tour the baroque city of Salzburg, the home of Mozart and the setting for The Sound of Music.

Celebrate the Year of the Red Fire Chicken in Style!

Where better to be to experience all the colour, culture and ritual of Chinese New Year than with a festive stay in China itself? Situated against the backdrop of the picturesque Mingsha Sand Dunes in Dunhuang, Gansu Province, The Silk Road Dunhuang Hotel captures the essence of the Tang Dynasty. With its unique architectural design; large rooftops, winding corridors of the Han and Tang dynasties, enclosed courtyards, rising mud walls synonymous with the north-western part of China and late Tang, Ming and Qing dynasties furniture, The Silk Road Dunhuang offers a stunningly traditional way to celebrate CNY with all the comforts of modern China to boot.
