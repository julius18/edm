Experience the Thai New Year in idyllic Phuket!

Songkran - the Thai New Year, is arguably the grandest and most important event on the Thai calendar. In more recent times the festival has grown to include a rolling water fight across towns, villages and cities - where locals and tourists alike splash each other with buckets and water pistols full of water!

Running from April 13th – 15th, you can experience Songkran with a stay at Karma Royal Boat Lagoon. Conveniently located on Phuket's east side, the resort is only 30 minutes from Phuket's international airport and boasts water views of Boat Lagoon, modern amenities and 17 self contained apartments with tiled floors and authentic Thai décor.

Be part of history at Thailand's 2017 Coronation Day

Although the Thai people are still saddened by the passing of His Majesty, King Bhumibol Adulyadej, his Coronation Day has previously been marked on the 5th of May.

With the new king, His Majesty Maha Vajiralongkorn, settling into his role, you can be part of this very special time in Thai history and stay at Karma Royal Bella Vista, located in Thailand's spiritual heartland, Chiang Mai. Call your Karma Concierge for more information.

Every flavour has a story at the Ubud Food Festival

This 12th - 14th of May savour the flavours of Indonesia at the Ubud Food Festival, Southeast Asia's largest culinary event.

This year's theme – 'Every flavour has a story' – is a celebration of the wealth of different cultures and regions that call Indonesia home.

Located just outside Ubud, Karma Mayura is a lush jungle retreat for food aficionados. After a day sampling delights from the world's top chefs, lounge in the infinity pool and sip on Balinese-inspired cocktails!

For lovers of Rosé – a Hungarian adventure

The Rosalia Rosé Festival, from the 12th to the 14th of May, is a celebration for lovers of Rosé, sparkling wines and Champagne. Set in the heart of Budapest, Hungary's capital, the festival sparkles with live music, free wine tastings, cellar door sales and fun for the whole family.

After a few days in the city, you might enjoy a relaxing countryside stay at the Abbázia Country Club, a hidden treasure featuring unspoiled nature, intimate villages nearby, authentic regional cuisine and an abundance of cultural attractions.
