KARMA MAYURA - THE CULTURAL HEART OF BALI

One of the latest additions to the Karma collection of worldwide resorts, Karma Mayura captures the spirit of a bygone Bali with its artful design and its bucolic location by the steep banks of a river. Discover the magic of Bali's cultural heartland - from the museums and art galleries of Ubud, to world-beating restaurants including the Michelin starred Mozaic, Balinese art forms from dance to gamelan orchestras and some of the prettiest countryside Bali has to offer - think stepped rice terraces, river gorges drenched in jungle and idyllic villages that have changed little in centuries. THE LANGUEDOC: DISCOVER THE SINGULAR DELIGHTS
OF THE FRENCH PYRENEES.

The Languedoc is one of the prettiest parts of the Pyrenees mountains in the south of France - a region of rolling hills punctuated by distinctive Mediterranean flora as well as orchards, vineyards and farmland. Just 15m from the famous town of Perpignan, Malibu Village brings together comfy apartments within a traditional pedestrian only village. It features no less than 6 swimming pools, as well as activities like basketball, volleyball, tennis and squash. The perfect base for a family holiday in one of France's most beloved regions - filled with historic cities, beaches and a very vivid Catalan culture that has survived intact to this day.

NORTHUMBRIA: WELCOME TO ENGLAND'S HISTORIC BORDERLANDS

Northumbria lies at the border between England and Scotland and is an area of outstanding natural beauty as well as enjoying a fascinating history. You'll spend happy hours wandering the rolling countryside of the Northumberland National Park, discovering the remnants of Hadrian's wall, castles, sampling local ales at the many traditional pubs. Activities include fishing, mountain biking and horse riding. You're home in Northumbria by staying in the Akeld Manor & Country Club - a haven of luxury featuring one - three bedroom units, a leisure centre featuring gym and pool.
