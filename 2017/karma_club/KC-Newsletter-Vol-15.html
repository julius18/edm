PHUKET - A TROPICAL ISLAND ICON



If you're looking for the sine qua non of tropical island paradises, Phuket has to be at the top of the list. It's not just the Thai island's stunning palm-studded beaches, the cerulean waters of the Andaman Sea or even the iridescent rainforests of the interior. Phuket also offers a cultural cornucopia that's just as captivating. You'll find myriad bustling markets that sell everything from intricately worked textiles to exotic fruit and vegetables to beautiful Buddhist iconography both old and new. There are ornate temples, streets filled with stalls selling some of the finest Asian food in the world. And no trip to Phuket is complete without a trip to the dazzling Phang Nga marine park, a maze of limestone karsts drenched in jungle, including the famous James Bond Island. Karma Royal Boat Lagoon is the perfect haven from which to explore the island, offering beautifully appointed two bedroom apartments, a selection of restaurants offering Thai, Italian and French cuisine. Book your autumn or winter break on one of the world's most iconic islands - direct flights are available from a wide range of international airports.

HIMALAYAN HEIGHTS - KARMA ROYAL'S RETREAT IN HISTORIC SHIMLA

The colonial British of the Raj era stumbled upon Shimla in the 19th century, fell in love with it and declared it their 'summer capital.' It's not difficult to appreciate why - there are few places in the world that can match it for beauty: the snow capped peaks of the Himalayas give onto iridescent green pastures broken by placid lakes reflecting the sky. As a settlement, Shimla has retained the charm of those bygone days and to this day is referred to as 'Queen of the Hills.' Royal Shimla takes full advantage of the dramatic topography and glorious mountain vistas, standing imposingly on a steep shoulder of land surrounded by evergreens and gazing out across a sweeping valley, with the enormous Himalayan massif serving as a backdrop. Karma Royal Himalaya spills down a dramatic hillside just a few kilometres outside Shimla, offering guests 27 luxurious rooms and a host of facilities including excellent fine dining restaurant Fairways, as well as introducing guests to activities from trekking to Yak safaris to ice skating to golf.

ISLAND SEAS RESORT - A LOVELY SLICE OF CARIBBEAN LIFE

The Caribbean has always had its own very special charm - and the Bahamas is among the region's most popular archipelagic states. You'll find Island Seas Resort in Freeport, Grand Bahama Island - a sprawling, well-maintained beach resort complete with its own private beach, hot tubs, gym, pool and spa. The biggest of the Bahamian islands Grand Bahama is a great place to experience the Caribbean, with a host of activities on offer from horse riding to fishing to sailing to snorkeling as well as sizzling nightlife. Don't miss the famous Junkanoo Carnival, which takes place each April and May in the Bahamas, with sound systems and carnival costumes and of course rum galore to be found everywhere!

DISCOVER CANADA'S CULTURAL CAPITAL WITH A TRIP TO
PARADISE CANYON GOLF RESORT

Lethbridge in Southern Alberta has long been regarded as one of Canada's cultural hubs thanks to its profusion of galleries, museums, theatres, music and other arts venues - indeed it was named Canada's cultural capital for the 2004-5 season. The Southern Alberta region enjoys pleasantly warm summers and mild winters thanks to the proximity of the Canadian Rockies. Lethbridge is southern Alberta's biggest city and a great jumping off point for outdoor activities in the Rockies. Settle into a relaxed rural rhythm with a stay at the stunning Paradise Canyon Golf Resort - the area's undisputed premier residence whether you're a golf fanatic, a culture vulture or a nature lover. Its 2 bedroom units are perfect for friends and families. You can get to Lethbridge from Calgary International Airport by plane, car or luxury bus.
