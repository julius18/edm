Bali's beautiful Tumpek Wayang

Bali may be known for its beautiful beaches and tropical climate, but what makes the Island of the Gods truly spectacular is its people and their colourful Hindu-animist culture.

During Tumpek Wayang, puppeteers from across the island gather at local temples to perform the 'Sapuh Leger', a traditional show that points to the coming together of the Gods, Shiva and Uma.

Located nearby many of Bali's most famous temples, Karma Jimbaran offers luxurious private pool villas with lush gardens, excellent dining, Karma Spa, pool and gym facilities.

An open air classic in Nuremberg, Germany

The Nuremberg Classic Open Air is the largest outdoor festival of classical music in Europe, drawing more than 160,000 visitors per year to two, free admission concerts at the end of July and the beginning of August.

Featuring the Nuremberg Symphonic Orchestra and the Staatsphilharmonie Nürnberg, the streets thrum to the sound of well-known classics, and more contemporary scores from stage and film.

Stay at: Karma Bavaria, Karma's alpine resort located above the stunning Lake Schliersee.

Bonderam, Goa's Flag Festival

On the tiny island of Divar, in Goa, the Bonderam Festival echoes with hints of Goa's colonial past.

Reminiscent of an Eddie Izzard sketch, the Portuguese sought to calm local property disputes through the cunning use of flags. In protest, the locals promptly used them for target practice! Every year, thousands of tourists come to experience what has become one of India's most colourful and enduring festivals.

Stay at: Karma Royal Benaulim, conveniently located near the pristine sands of Benaulim Beach.

Red wine lovers flock to Hungary

Egri Bikavér, or Bull's Blood, is a full-bodied, bolshy red wine from Eger - Hungary's second largest city. It's so notable that every year over 40 local cellars fling open their doors for the annual Bull's Blood Festival.

Joined by some of Europe's top chefs - who craft delicious delicacies to complement the wine's acidic tones, the three-day festival also includes live folk, jazz and choral music.

Stay at: Club Abbázia, located close to the famous thermal lake of Heviz.
