DISCOVER THE CULTURAL HEART OF VIETNAM WITH THE OPENING OF KARMA CÂY TRE THIS WEEK!


On 3rd of September, Karma Cây Tre, our inaugural property in Vietnam, opens its doors. The latest addition to the acclaimed Karma collection of resorts is located in Vietnam's cultural heartland in the town of Hoi An, part of a narrow strip of land that plays host to some of the country's most famous landmarks. Hoi An itself is a UNESCO world heritage site famed for its historically important architecture as well as its culinary offerings, which include a huge variety of dishes with both Vietnamese and Chinese influences - from classics such as Goi Cuon spring rolls to crunchy fried crab cakes to delicious black sesame pudding. Meanwhile the whole region is a tapestry of picture perfect beaches, ancient temples and rolling countryside. Discover South East Asia's most exciting up and coming destination at one of the country's finest resorts! For more info contact your personal Karma Concierge.

KARMA REEF - A CAST AWAY LOVE STORY

Few things could be more romantic than a castaway wedding on a palm - fringed tropical island surrounded by the cerulean waters of the Indian Ocean. This is precisely what Karma Reef Gili Meno offers. Set on a tiny atoll between the islands of Bali and Lombok, Karma Reef is the epitome of barefoot luxury - a mix of five star villas and glamping tents, just metres from the beach with views that are all ocean! Karma Reef is set up to deliver your dream wedding, with bespoke services covering everything from your wedding cake, decorations & hair and make up to photo & video coverage, music & entertainment and even yacht charters! Add to this a host of extra curricular activities from diving to sea kayaking to thalassic spas and you have the makings of a match truly made in heaven!

UNDERWATER WONDERS WITH Karma Royal Boat Lagoon, Phuket

Immerse yourself in an underwater wonderland of vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish in one of the world's favourite dive destinations. Karma Royal Boat Lagoon, Phuket is situated in one of southern Thailand's most celebrated islands - one surrounded by world-class dive sites and dive operators. Plus there's more than enough après-dive activities to keep you happy, including boutique shopping, dining, Phuket's pretty Old Town and a spirited nightlife! The resort itself is the perfect tropical sanctuary, with a host of facilities including pool, games room, library and restaurant including bakery, plus of course your own spacious 2 bedroom apartment to relax in.
