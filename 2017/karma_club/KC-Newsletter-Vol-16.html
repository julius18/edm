LEARN THE ART OF BALINESE DANCE AT KARMA JIMBARAN!



Travel is all about new experiences - which is why we love to invite our members to get up close and personal with the fascinating cultures in many of our destinations. Bali offers a particularly rich tapestry - and amongst the island's most celebrated art forms is Balinese Dance. At Karma Jimbaran, we connect guests with some of the island's foremost dancers, who bring to life the many different forms of Balinese dance, explaining the incredibly subtle nuances of movement and expression and revealing the sacred aspect of the art form. It's just one of a raft of fascinating activities you can enjoy at the acclaimed resort, including visits to the famous seafood market, cooking classes, temple visits, beach excursions, fine dining and more…

DISCOVER THE SECRETS OF THAI CUISINE AT KARMA ROYAL BELLA VISTA

Chiang Mai, Thailand's northern capital, is a beautiful and fascinating destination. Set among verdant rolling mountains dotted with the villages of Indigenous Hmong peoples, it is both a sophisticated modern city, yet one laden with history. It also has a very unique approach to cooking, which you can discover yourself through Karma Royal Bella Vista's cooking classes. The resort's master chefs will share with you the secret ingredients and combinations that make Thai cooking so uniquely sophisticated. Visit local markets to source ingredients before trying your hand at some Thai classics. A great addition to your Chiang Mai holiday, all in a serene resort with a host of facilities including swimming pools, tennis court, spa, gym, restaurants and private cottages drenched in flowers.
