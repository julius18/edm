Announcing our new resort on the Greek island of Crete!

As part of Karma Group’s continued expansion and commitment to bringing our members and guests the best holiday experiences on earth, we are extremely proud to announce the acquisition of a new property on the Greek island of Crete, just outside the beating heart of the Crete’s own thriving arts, fashion and culture centre - Agios Nikolaos!

Located only 5kms from the town, Karma Minoan rests between rugged mountains and the sea. Karma Minoan will be comprised of a selection of hotel apartments and one and two bedroom suites along with pool, bar, restaurant, library, spa and kids club. Each of the property’s 26 holiday apartments offer panoramic views of Mirabello Bay, Agios Nikolaos and the surrounding mountains.

The resort is undergoing refurbishment and will open its doors to members in 2018, stay tuned!

Bounce into Easter at Karma Bavaria!

For a very different Easter celebration, join us at Karma Bavaria this 14th-17th of April 2017 for a basketful of family fun! Over four days, the hillsides and laneways of the picturesque Bavarian town of Schliersee come alive with traditional Easter celebrations.

Book now and enjoy a traditional Good Friday dinner and Easter Sunday dinner, along with an Easter Egg hunt for the whole family!

In between feasts, take the time to explore Munich, Bavaria’s capital and one of the prettiest cities anywhere in the world. Have a stein or two at a traditional beer hall, or explore Munich’s rich folkloric past.

Ski Mount Hutt – New Zealand’s top ski destination

Attention lovers of cold days and powder! If you haven’t skied Mt Hutt, you’re missing out! With the largest skiable area on the South Island, a ‘magic carpet’ lift for beginners, good intermediate and advanced runs and access to a wealth of stunning off-piste areas, it’s easy to see why Mt Hutt has been voted New Zealand’s best ski destination. Ski season kicks off in June and runs until October and Karma has your accommodation needs covered! Stay at the Mount Hutt Lodge, conveniently located near Springfield station. To get there take the Transalpine express - truly one of the world’s most majestic railway journeys!

A springtime medieval marvel in Italy’s Umbria

Spring has sprung and Italy’s agricultural heartland, Umbria, is in full bloom! There’s no better place for a relaxing seasonal break than the Castello Izzalini – a stately and proud medieval hamlet located nearby the town of Todi.

Throw open the curtains of your sumptuously appointed apartment to views of the Tiber River valley and the castle grounds - with a myriad of different trees and gardens bursting with springtime growth. After breakfast, take a tour of the surrounds and sample the renowned flavour of Umbrian olive oil and locally produced cheeses – it’s a food lover’s dream stay!
