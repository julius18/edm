Crete’s summer-long party

If you could pick only one Greek island to visit this summer, which one would it be?

Where can you spend the morning splashing about in the crystal clear blue of the Aegean, the afternoons sampling robust and quaffable local wines and the evenings rubbing shoulders with Europe’s A-list at opulently decked-out beach clubs?

It has to be Crete.

There’re so many cultural, food and sporting events on offer you’ll have a hard time choosing. Dance up a storm at the B.I.G. Party Ierapetra Experience - easily the biggest beach party in Crete, or for something a bit more refined take a drive to Agios Nikolaos and discover the latest in avant-garde art, theatre and dance as part of the town's “Mirabello” arts festival.

For a truly authentic Cretan holiday experience, stay at Karma Royal Villea. Located in the Ierapetra area and set amid the cool surrounds of traditional olive groves and thriving bougainvillea-filled gardens, the resort offers comfortable, bright apartments and a wealth of facilities.

Start a new Easter tradition

Looking for a very different type of Easter break this year? Karma Reef has the answer! Join us on the barefoot island paradise of Gili Meno this 14th to16th of April for Karma Reef's Easter weekend, featuring international DJ sensation, Dan Cook..

Easter Saturday kicks off with a barefoot beach BBQ, featuring fresh-caught local grilled seafood and meats bursting all the flavour and spice of Indonesian cuisine! On Easter Sunday join us for a sumptuous Easter brunch followed an egg hunt for the whole family!

Dig into a paddock-to-plate experience like no other

Attention lovers of food! Head to the Felton Food Festival this 26th of March 2017 for a paddock to plate experience on a grand scale. You’ll be stunned at the variety of fresh boutique food on offer, all grown - or raised - in the rich black soils of Queensland’s Felton Valley.

Afterwards, head up the coast for a night or two at the Allegra Hervey Bay. The resort’s luxurious, spotlessly modern apartments are the perfect place from which to explore the jaw-dropping natural beauty of the Great Barrier Reef.

Moorish Marbella - The Costa del Sol’s new-old wonder town

Fashionable, established, polished – the Spanish town Marbella, located along Andalusia’s famous Costa del Sol - is for lovers of style. Marbella offers visitors a little bit of everything – from the history and cool shade of the Moorish old town to stylish beaches, upmarket restaurants and an edgy arts scene.

Sounds enticing? Ask your Karma Concierge about Club Marbella, located in the heart of town. This recently refurbished resort offers guests modern and comfortable accommodation, large family friendly pool areas and even crazy golf for the kids!
