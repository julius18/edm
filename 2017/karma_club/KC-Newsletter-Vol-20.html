SERENE IN SANUR - THE SOFT SIDE OF BALI…



In the seventies and eighties, Sanur was the undisputed jet set destination in Bali, attracting celebrity glitterati like David Bowie, Mick Jagger and Jerry Hall. These days Sanur has a very different vibe - it is the peaceful Yin to its Yang cousins Kuta and Seminyak on the west coast of the island, where visitors flock to party. Sanur is all about long strolls along the market lined beachfront, meals at legendary restaurants like Kafe Batu Jimbar and visits to the little offshore island of Nusa Lembongan. And of course, many of Bali's iconic sites are just a short drive away - from the volcanoes of Kintamani to temples like Tanah Lot or Tampak Siring. Karma Royal Sanur blends contemporary style and amenities with a classically artful Balinese design aesthetic - its smart one and two bedroom apartments set in lush gardens around a pool. Your perfect haven in Bali.

KARMA BAVARIA - RETREAT INTO NATURE WHATEVER THE SEASON

Whatever the season, the Bavarian Alps are a Mecca for nature lovers and those drawn to the spectacular mountain landscapes. As autumn settles over Europe, the valleys and mountains are awash with changing colors as trees fade from green to deep red and russet brown. And once the snow settles, Karma Bavaria becomes a winter wonderland, the highlands covered in snow, drawing skiers and snowboarders to their famous slopes. But it's not all about whizzing down the pistes. Don your snowshoes or cross country skis and try some winter trekking too. Enjoy the famous sled racing contests that local farmers have been doing for generations. Walk the shores of Lake Schliersee and look up at the spectacular Karma Bavaria resort, your holiday home, before heading back up there for a pre - dinner aperitif.

FOR A QUINTESSENTIAL TASTE OF NEW SOUTH WALES
HEAD TO PORT MACQUARie

Just under 400km north of Australia's capital Sydney is Port Macquarie a pretty town at the mouth of Hastings River that's a world away from the bustling metropolis. This is where you come to get the most out of New South Wales' incredible natural environments. Think endless stretches of golden sand, world-class surfing, boating trips along spectacular coastlines, fishing, family barbecues and more. Ultiqa @ Village Resort is set in a private lagoon and offers a wide range of amenities and activities - including 3 bedroom apartments perfect for families, an indoor sports complex, sauna spa and gym plus winery tours, river cruises and even camel rides! Discover the wild yet gentle side of New South Wales at Port Macquarie!
