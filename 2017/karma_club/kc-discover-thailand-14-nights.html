Chiang Mai & Phuket - 14 Night Discovery

Discover Thailand's 'capital in the north,' Chiang Mai - a city that dates back to the 13th century and that offers a rich mix of experiences, whether you wish to explore its fascinating Buddhist temples, discover the distinctive northern cuisine, or immerse in the breathtaking natural surroundings. Chiang Mai also has a sophisticated contemporary feel a rich tradition of the arts, world class cuisine, shopping and nightlife. Little wonder that Trip Advisor readers voted it among the top 25 destinations in the world! Phuket is just as iconic - when it comes to tropical island destinations, few can offer such a rich tapestry of sights, sounds and experiences - from its gorgeous palm-fringed beaches to its sizzling nightlife, ornate temples and interior rainforests. Enjoy sunset yoga on the beach, dive colourful coral gardens and take a boat trip to the iconic James Bond Island - a fantastical tower of limestone in one of Thailand's most beautiful marine parks. All topped off with incredible culinary offerings and that legendary Thai hospitality!
