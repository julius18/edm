Karma Carcassonne Set to Open Next Year


Carcassonne is one of the jewels of France's many famous destinations - second only to Paris in popularity. Its appeal is easily explained - a spectacular citadel that has been preserved in pristine condition, set on a hilltop with breathtaking vistas across classic rolling French countryside. It is here in this most Gallic of settings that the brand new Karma Carcassonne will open next year. To visit Carcassonne is to be transported to a different time - for the region's importance as a strategic stronghold dates back as far as 100BC, when the Romans built a fortress here. Today, it is one of France's leading tourism destinations and you'll find no shortage of shops, eateries and other attractions in the near vicinity - plus it is easy to get to wherever you are in Europe. Karma Club members will soon be able to enjoy all the magic of Carcassonne from the luxurious confines of our stunning new resort.

Karma Chakra - Discover Kerala's Breathtaking Interior

Karma Chakra nestles on the banks of Vembanad, the largest lake in Kerala, famed for its scenic beauty, wetland flora and fauna and traditional houseboats. Partially built over the lake, the tranquil resort features breezy pavilions and opulent guest rooms that take full advantage of the serene prospect of palm-fringed waters. Occupying more than one and a half hectares of beautifully landscaped gardens, the resort features its own private jetty, while quaint wooden bridges and paths connect different areas of the resort. Discover another side of Kerala beyond the famous palm fringed beaches!
