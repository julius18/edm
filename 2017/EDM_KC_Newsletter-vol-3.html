A GOOD SPORT

Karma named official partner of Perth’s Western Forces Super Rugby Franchise

Welcome to volume 3 of the Karma Club newsletter and we’re kicking off (pun intended!) with the news that Karma Resorts has just been named Official Resort Partner of the Perth based Western Force Super Rugby franchise, a leading Rugby Union team that plays in the high profile international Super Rugby competition. Karma is also an Official Jersey Partner for the second season running as well as sponsoring this year’s Man of the Match award, the lucky winner of which will enjoy a week’s stay at flagship resort Karma Kandara in Bali. Mark Sindeberry, the team’s Executive Officer said “I’m excited about the partnership with Karma and look forward to a strong future. The partnership adds value to the rugby community by providing great holiday experiences to fans, players and our junior force players through the Dads & Lads weekend at Karma Rottnest.” Yet another facet of the global Karma lifestyle that connects our staff, members and partners!

TOTALLY SCILLY - BRITAIN’S BEST KEPT SECRET

The Scilly Isles crouch low & green just 28 miles off Cornwall’s southwest coast – an idyllic archipelago that recalls the continent and whose unique ecosystems are matched by a rich history dating back as far as the Phoenicians, Ancient Greeks & Romans…Rearing cliffs and sparkling white beaches give onto turquoise waters that are home to seals and a host of endemic sea birds as well as rare species from North America and Siberia.

Whether you have a penchant for island hopping or kayaking, trekking or bird watching, horse riding or simply immersing in the island’s laid-back culture and history, our world class rustic resort, Karma St Martin’s is the perfect way to discover Britain’s best kept secret

DESTINATION OF THE WEEK:
PHUKET – THE PARADISE ISLAND THAT NEVER SLUMBERS!

Thailand’s southern islands are rightly counted among the most desirable in the world for travellers, with their glittering palm fringed beaches, rich Buddhist culture, heady nightlife offerings and sheer tropical beauty. Phuket is amongst the most famous of this archipelago, offering the perfect mix of stunning natural environments, exhilarating activities like diving, surfing and sailing and world class boutiques, restaurants and bars & clubs.

Make sure to explore the legendary Phang Nga Bay, with its gorgeous limestone monoliths clad in jungle, including the much-photographed James Bond Island. The Karma Royal Lighthouse Villas including a tranquil Karma Spa facility are your perfect gateway to experiencing this iconic tropical island…
