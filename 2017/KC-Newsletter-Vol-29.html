ROTTNEST ISLAND - WITH 63 BAYS & BEACHES TO CHOOSE FROM,
FESTIVE SEASON NEVER FELT SO TROPICAL!



It's not difficult to understand why Rottnest is one of the premier holiday destinations in Western Australia. Just a short boat ride from Perth, it boasts 63 exquisitely beautiful bays and beaches, all lapped by the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. Not to mention, a fascinating history and a host of activities to choose from! Karma Rottnest is quite simply the best way to experience this unique island - a perfect holiday season retreat, with a range of accommodations that will suit romantic couples or large groups of families and friends. Enjoy sunset cruises within sight of the towers of Perth, or simply relax around the pool sipping on a signature cocktail. As for the island itself, there is no shortage of exciting activities to take part in. Diving and snorkeling are popular pastimes, with both reefs and wrecks to explore. Other popular activities include cycling, tennis, golf bowls and stand up paddle-boarding. Happy holidays, tropical style!

BAVARIAN FESTIVE FEASTS & CUSTOMS

Northern Europe is surely one of the most authentic places in which to experience the winter holidays - and few regions can match Bavaria's magical atmosphere. It's also home to some very singular customs and seasonal dishes! For instance, it's traditional on Christmas Eve to feast on Carp roasted in butter, served with beer - a Franconian tradition with a thousand year history. More familiar traditions include baking hard, spicy biscuits for Christmas. And while Santa Claus is definitely a fixture here, so is his nemesis Krampus - a demon that roams the streets ready to catch naughty children. Very fearsome he looks with his horns and furry coat! Karma Bavaria is the perfect place to spend festive season with family and friends - you can learn to prepare classic Bavarian dishes as well as enjoying tours of ancient villages and around lake Schliersee, with lots of mulled wine to keep you warm!

A GOURMET GUIDE TO PARIS

There can be few cities as romantic as Paris - and perhaps none that can challenge its preeminence as a culinary capital. Here are four restaurants that should be on any true gourmand's bucket list:
1. L'Atelier de Ben : A little restaurant that defines classic French food done slowly and with love. 2. Le Carre : A Paris institution - contemporary layout but the food is as classic as it gets.
3. Bistrot Côté Seine : Right by the river, a tiny bistro that packs a huge culinary punch!
4. Temps d'Met : A taste of Provence in the heart of Paris.

Stay at the stunning Novotel Porte de La Chappelle and explore Paris' restaurants at your leisure.

AN OLYMPIAN ESCAPE OVERLOOKING THE MEDITERRANEAN IN CYPRUS

With its Olympian setting high above the Mediterranean Sea near Paphos, the 5 star Aphrodite Hills Resort does indeed feel like a place built for the gods. Its relaxed opulence is immediately calming - though there's no shortage of activities to take part in, from world-class 18-hole golf course, to tennis, spa, fitness facilities and even a Greco-Roman Thermae! This is one of the prettiest regions in Cyprus and you'll want to explore the nearby beaches - don't miss the Zias Beach Club, a short drive away.
