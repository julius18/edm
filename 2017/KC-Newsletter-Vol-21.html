VIETNAM - 4000 YEARS OF CULTURE MEETS NATURE'S TIMELESS BEAUTY


Vietnam is one of southeast Asia's most exciting destinations as it makes its presence felt on the world tourism stage. It has so much going for it: It's cultural origins are among the oldest in southeast Asia, whilst over the years it has absorbed a wide variety of influences - particularly Chinese. UNESCO World Heritage site distils these many cultural elements. You'll discover quaint Chinese shop fronts, temples and tea warehouses as well as an abundance of French colonial design, an iconic Japanese bridge and local Vietnamese tube houses. Venture beyond its borders and you're in a rural paradise of iridescent rice paddies, purling rivers and white sand beaches lapped by the aquamarine waters of the South China Sea. It's this mix of cosmopolitan nature and culture - not to mention a similar cornucopia of local food offerings - that make Hoi An so special. And set on its outskirts by a purling river, just one kilometre from the nearest beach, our latest resort Karma Cây Tre is the perfect tranquil retreat from which to discover it all!

KARMA JIMBARAN - THE DEFINITION OF LUXURY LIVING IN THE TROPICS

Set in one of Bali's most exclusive neighbourhoods, Karma Jimbaran is one of the island's most well loved and admired luxury resorts. Its villa residences reflect the Balinese garden compound design that has become a global design classic - connected by paths that meander through lush equatorial gardens filled with fragrant frangipani. Within its walls are a destination restaurant, award winning Karma Spa, Kid's Club, Bar with one of the best wine selections on the island, plus pool and gym. Jimbaran Bay is just metres from the resort - don't miss its legendary sunsets, best experiences beachside. The staff are on hand to help arrange an itinerary that will bring you the very best of the Bali - from helicopter trips to temple tours, mountain biking volcanoes to exploring the island's famous arts and crafts. Karma Jimbaran offers an authentic Bali experience for everyone from couples to large groups and families. Contact your Karma Concierge and discover for yourself what makes it so special!

WAPATO POINT - EXPERIENCE THE BEAUTY OF WASHINGTON STATE

Set on the edge of beautiful Lake Chelan in the heart of Washington State Wapato Point brings together spacious one to three bedroom condominiums with a host of amenities including a luxurious Jacuzzi. Not to be confused with the US capital, Washington State in the Pacific Northwest is famed for its gorgeous landscapes - mountains, forests and lakes abound, making it a paradise for outdoor activities like hiking, rafting, fishing and even cross country skiing in the winter. It's also home to one of the US's most culturally influential cities - Seattle (also home to the nearest international airport), which is well worth a visit.

THE ROMANCE OF THE SOUTH PACIFIC AT CLUB BALI HAI MOOREA

Its namesake may be a few thousand miles away in southeast Asia, but Bali Hai Moorea is an island paradise that's just as captivating. Think Tahiti and images of pearl white beaches and electric blue waters immediately spring to mind. And rightly so! Bali Hai Moorea is built right over these pellucid waters, so you can wake up and slip straight into the ocean. The resort has its own small beaches as well as a swimming pool. You'll find brightly coloured coral reefs right beside your cottage and you can book dolphin and whale watching excursions at the front desk.
