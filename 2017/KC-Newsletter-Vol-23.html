CANDIDASA - BALI'S LITTLE EAST COAST SECRET


While the famous resort enclaves of Kuta, Seminyak, Sanur and Ubud are the big draw for many visitors of Bali, the island is home to some stunning destinations that are not so firmly on the travel radar. One of the most tranquil is Candidasa on Bali's east coast. A somnolent little harbor town, Candidasa is hemmed by dramatic mountain landscapes, while its coastal waters offer world class snorkeling and diving. Be sure to get directions to Pasir Putih - one of the island's few white beaches, set in a pretty cove with a gentle lagoon to swim in. Set in lush tropical gardens, Karma Royal Candidasa's villa apartments gaze out over the Indian Ocean, while guests can access the beach directly from the expansive lap pool. Trek the lush hills around the resort, try some river rafting, check out the Bali Safari & Marine Park or simply wile the afternoon away on a sun lounger, cocktail in hand!

CHIANG MAI - THAILAND'S FASCINATING NORTHERN CAPITAL

Discover Thailand's 'capital in the north,' Chiang Mai - a city that dates back to the 13th century and that offers a rich tapestry of experiences. Explore its elegant Theravadan Buddhist temples, discover the distinctive northern cuisine including the iconic Khao Soi - a yellow noodle dish in a rich coconut curry sauce. Explore the surrounding countryside, with visits to nearby villages of the local Hmong culture. Chiang Mai also has a sophisticated contemporary scene including a rich tradition of the arts, world-class modern cuisine, shopping and nightlife. Karma Royal Bella Vista is a full service resort ideal for families, groups as well as couples, featuring 2 bedroom cottages that mix classic and modern Thai design features, with additional features including on site restaurants, swimming pools and Karma Spa - the perfect place from which to explore this fascinating highland region.

INDIA'S KODAIKANAL - SWITZERLAND OF THE EAST

The Kodaikanal region of Tamil Nadu India is characterized by ranks of breathtaking forested hills among which nestle placid lakes. Ensconced in this bucolic landscape beside one such lake is the aptly named Sterling Kodai - By the Lake Resort. The resort spills down a hillside and is surrounded by fragrant eucalyptus trees and other evergreens including conifer and shoal forest. It's not difficult to see why the region is referred to as a Switzerland of the East. This is a resort for nature loving families with a host of treks that range from gentle to challenging - and once you've had your fill of the glorious natural surroundings, check out the local chocolate factory or ease away any kinks or tightness with a visit to the Prana Spa.

A GORGEOUS WILDERNESS IN SOUTHERN TAIWAN

Kentington Resort is located in a setting that some may not associate with Taiwan - a Western style ranch set in 40 acres of lush green valley that forms a part of a protected national park. If you love and your loved ones are all about a wide range of activities, then Kentington Resort is for you. Within the resort you'll find everything from Billiards and Arcade games to swimming pools, a bowling range and even an archery range! Venture into the surrounding countryside and there's great fishing to be enjoyed, mountain biking, nearby zoos and cross-country running. The perfect way to immerse in your holiday!
