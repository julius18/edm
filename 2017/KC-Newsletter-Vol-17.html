VIETNAM - EXPLORE ONE OF SOUTHEAST ASIA'S
MOST ANCIENT & CAPTIVATING CULTURES



Vietnamese culture has one of the longest histories in southeast Asia and can be traced all the way back to the ancient bronze age Dong Son culture. But a succession of outside influences has helped make the country what it is today - from a long period of Chinese control where Confucianism took root, to colonization by the French and of course most recently, communism. Many of these cultural attributes can be viewed in the fascinating town of Hoi An, a UNESCO World Heritage famed particularly for its Chinese and Japanese influences. Indeed, the whole region around Hoi An is a rich repository of culture - visible not just in the sites of special interest, but in the everyday life of the people - from the delicious street food available everywhere to Daoist and Buddhist temples. Our new resort Karma Cây Tre is situated in the heart of this rich cultural tapestry - a peaceful riverside haven from which to explore one of southeast Asia's most captivating destinations.

FINLAND - WELCOME TO THE WILD YET OH SO CIVILISED NORTH!

As Autumn begins to take hold in the northern hemisphere, it's a wonderful time to visit Scandinavia, as nature puts on her most colorful display. Ensconced between Sweden, Norway, Russia and Estonia and bordering the Baltic, Finland offers the quintessential Scandinavian experience - think saunas, dramatic landscapes, forests, lakes, good design, Aurora Borealis, smartly designed cities and of course the Moomins…Your holiday home in Finland is Holiday Club Katinkulta, a sprawling lakeside resort in the north east of the country, with private cottages, spa and even a golf course. It's perfect for couples and families alike and there's a wide range of activities to enjoy in the local area from cross country skiing to hiking to fishing.

MALLORCA - A BALEARIC LOVE AFFAIR

There's something very special about the long drawn out summers of Spain's Balearic Islands, which stay balmy well into November and even December. Mallorca has long been a favorite destination, thanks to its profusion of beaches, crystal blue bays, dramatic craggy coastline, vast array of restaurants, nightclubs and boutique shopping options. Ona Aucanada Resort is situated in the pretty town of Palma de Mallorca and features luxurious pool villas and apartments, gym, pool, jacuzzi, restaurant and ocean views. Enjoy windsurfing, snorkeling and fishing, or land based activities from golf to volleyball. The perfect way to extend your summer fun!
