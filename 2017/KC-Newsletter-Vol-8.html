PRESTIGIOUS MUNICH MUSIC FESTIVAL ARRIVING END OF AUGUST

One of the world's most renowned classical music festivals takes place in Munich - a spiritual home of western classical. The ARD International Music Competition is the biggest in Germany and enjoys international prestige. Between 29th of August and 16th of September, visitors to Munich have a unique opportunity to witness an array of virtuoso musicians performing in venues throughout the city - plus of course the Munich Philharmonic. Many of the most famous musicians in the world playing instruments from harps to French horns, violins to oboes, cut their teeth here, where the next generation gets its chance to step into the limelight and prove their mettle to the watching world. Our stunning Alpine retreat, Karma Bavaria is just 45 minutes away on the tranquil shores of Lake Schliersee, making for a perfect mix of nature & music! For more information on ARD you can go to: www.br.de
