Find the spirit of Thailand in Chiang Mai


Visit amazing Chiang Mai during Songkran, the traditional Thai New Year festival, held on the 13th of April. People flock to the city for the festival's famous water fight, where the young (and young at heart) let loose in a carefree celebration by splashing water on each other.

The Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, just outside the city, is beautiful to behold. The temple's Stupa rises above the other buildings in a glittering tower of gold. The views will take your breath away!

When in Chiang Mai, stay at Karma Royal Bella Vista. Designed like a small village, cobbled pathways meander between the resort's intimate cottages, two pools and landscaped gardens. Contact your personal Karma Club Concierge now!

A cosy Valentine's Day escape in snowy Bavaria

There's nothing quite like the romantic splendour of the Bavarian mountains, especially towards the tail end of winter, and there's no better place to explore this amazing destination than with a stay at Karma Bavaria, Karma's own cosy mountain retreat.

During the day, take the time to explore the beautiful spa town of Schliersee, Germany, by horse-drawn carriage, or get active hiking one of the many nature trails throughout Bavaria's snow-capped mountains. Seeking a more measured pace? Enjoy a meandering stroll along the shores of lake Schliersee. In the evening, snuggle up your room and sample some of the best wines from Germany and abroad, or dine at Karmasee restaurant where our talented chefs will prepare a special meal just for you. As night deepens, pamper yourselves by unwinding in Karma Bavaria's own sauna, Jacuzzi and heated pool.

Club Casablanca - a very Spanish time in Tenerife

Located along the idyllic northwest coast of Tenerife you'll find Club Casablanca, a stunning holiday-style resort in the heart of Puerto de la Cruz.

By day, play on the black sands of Jardin Beach, or take a day trip to explore the high mountain massifs that nestle between ancient volcanic peaks. By night, sit and watch the world go by while dining at Bar Dinámico, in Plaza Charco.

Back at Club Casablanca, you'll be impressed by the friendly smiles of the staff and the resort's prime location, situated in the fashionable La Paz district. Onsite facilities include a swimming pool with children's area, bar and restaurant. You'll also find a sauna and gymnasium nearby.

Where the sun never crosses Finnish line
- Holiday Club Hannunkivi, Finland

During the long, long days of summer in Western Finland, it's quite common to find yourself in daylight… at midnight. Once you get your head around that, you'll be even more excited about a stay at Holiday Club Hannunkivi. This traditionally decked-out Finnish resort welcomes you to Finland's wild heart, where nature is all about and a relaxing outdoors holiday is but a step away. Depending on when you visit, the surrounds are perfect for wild fishing, hunting, berry picking, mushroom-picking, skiing and hiking!
